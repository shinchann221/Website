Digital and Analytics

Pest and Yield Analysis

India has ~30 million farmers who own smartphones, which is expected to grow 3 times by 2020. Digital farming and connected farm services can impact 70 million Indian farmers. A technology- enabled approach to farming management that observes, measures, and analyzes the needs of individual fields and crops and uses the data in a smart way to optimize farming operations. Good digital usage and analytics helps in better output prediction and quality maximization and helps in risk management. It’ll help to enhance preventive care and increase producer-consumer satisfaction. AI-enabled robots for harvesting can lead to huge cost savings by reducing the need for approximately 4 agricultural laborers per acre of land.
