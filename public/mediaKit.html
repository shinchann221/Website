About Neem Tree Agro Solutions-

We're a SAAS based data driven AgTech company established in 2018 with a mission to support farmers with use of technology like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Data Science. We’re incorporated as a private limited company under the Companies Act, 2013. Neem Tree provides a data driven crop enhancement system, NT-Kisan. It facilitates the decision making process and helps farmers grow efficiently and grow more subsequently increasing the income of the small and marginalised farmers.
